BROMO, IJEN & BATU VILLAGE TOUR
4 Days 3 Nights (4D / 3N)






TOUR ITINERARY:


Day 1 = Surabaya or Malang - Cemara Lawang (Bromo area)
  • Picking up in Surabaya or Malang Airport or train station or hotel
  • Transport to Cemara Lawang, length of travel is about 4 hours and then check in to the hotel
  • Check in to the hotel at Bromo Permai or Cafe Lava or Cemara Indah (* standard room with breakfast, hot and cold shower)
  • Relaxing program


Day 2 = Bromo Tour - Bondowoso
  • At 3:30 a.m., You have to get ready in the reception room or restaurant for Bromo tour
  • By using a hartop 4wd jeep, You will start the bromo tour from the hotel to Penanjakan Peak 1 in 1 hour driving, to see the sunrise and the scenery around Mount Bromo from the highest peak
  • You will enjoy this view until around 6 o'clock in the morning
  • Afterwards, by using a 4wd jeep, You will visit the sea of sand and jeep will be parked in the parking lot of visitors to Mount Bromo crater
  • To go to The Bromo crater, You can walk to the up stair of Mount Bromo for 30 minutes, or can rent a horse
  • Then You use the down stairs to get to the edge of the crater of Mount Bromo
  • You can walk on the edge of Mount Bromo but please be careful
  • After that You go back to the jeep parking lot and travel back to the hotel
  • You have to take a bath in the private bathroom which has been provided by the hotel, so You are ready for breakfast
  • The breakfast program was finished around 9:30 a.m., and preparations to check out at 10 a.m.
  • Traveling for 5 hours to Bondowoso City
  • Check in to the hotel at Ijen View or Palem Hotel * Standard room with breakfast, hot and cold shower, and aircon
  • Free program
  • At 11:30 p.m., You leave the hotel and travel for 2 hours to Paltuding (starting point for climbing to Mount Ijen)



Day 3 = Bondowoso - Ijen Trekking Program - Batu Village
  • Arrive at Paltuding at 1:30 am
  • Accompanied by Ijen's local guide, You buy an entrance ticket and then start trekking until You reach the top of Mount Ijen after 2 hours
  • At 3:30 a.m., You arrive at the peak of Ijen and from here You can see the natural phenomenon of "Blue Fire"
  • You will see Blue Fire (terms and conditions may apply)
  • You have to use gas mask
  • Around 6 a.m., You returned to the peak of Ijen, and trekking back to Paltuding
  • Arriving at Paltuding back around 8 - 8:30 a.m., and then returning to the hotel, traveling for 2 hours
  • Take a bath and then breakfast around 11 a.m.
  • At 12 pm, You check out and travel back to Surabaya for 6 hours, so arriving in Batu around 8 pm (terms and conditions may apply)
  • Check in to the De-Daunan or De-View or Batu Permai or Grand Pujon View Hotel * Standard room with breakfast, hot and cold shower
  • Break time


Day 4 = Batu Village Tour - Surabaya
  • After breakfast, You check out the hotel and start the Batu tour to: 1. Pick Apple Tourism 2. Coban Rondo Waterfall and Labirin Park 3. Feather Hill (selfie spot) and Flower Garden 4. Omah Kayu (Wooden House) 5. Paragliding in Mount Banyak (for those who like paragliding)
  • At 2 o'clock in the afternoon, You leave Batu Village and return to Surabaya, traveling for 4 hours, so arriving in Surabaya around 6 pm (terms and conditions may apply)
  • Trip complete

Tour Includes
  • Transport accommodation for 4 days and 3 nights, including driver, fuel, toll fees, and parking
  • 4wd Jeep for Bromo Tour
  • 1 night stay at Bromo Permai or Cafe Lava or Cemara Indah Hotel Standard room with breakfast, hot shower and cold water
  • 1 night stay at Ijen View or Palem Hotel Standard room with breakfast, hot and cold shower, aircon
  • 1 night stay at de-Daunan or De-View or Batu Permai or Grand Pujon View Hotel Standard room with breakfast, hot shower and cold water
  • Local guide Ijen
  • Mineral water
  • 3x breakfast (hotel)


Tour Excludes

  • Lunch and dinner
