Tips for Traveling to Mount Bromo

In order to make your tour to Mount Bromo smooth and pleasant, the following tips should be tried:




  • Plan your trip carefully, including physical, financial and financial preparation.

  • Choose the best time to depart, you should visit the tour in the dry season because the charm of the sunrise is more perfect.

  • If you plan to stay at the inn, then make sure to book the inn in advance.

  • Choose a safe transportation because the path is full of climbs, make sure the vehicle used is in a fit condition.

  • Clothing accessories must be adapted to the weather conditions in the cold mountains, so be sure to bring a thick jacket, long pants, gloves, warm headgear and other necessary equipment.

  • Also be sure to wear comfortable shoes, flashlights, masks and glasses to protect yourself when you are on a stretch of desert or caldera.

  • Don't forget to bring a camera to capture exciting moments and also the breathtaking view of Mount Bromo.

  • Finally, keep the attitude and behavior so as not to encounter any obstacles during the tourist sites of Mount Bromo.
